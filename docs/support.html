Home Health

Support

Home Health helps you and your household keep your place clean. Each task slowly “gets dirty” on its own schedule; finish it and it resets to spotless. A single home‑health score and a daily streak show how your whole home is doing.

How do tasks work?

Every task repeats on a schedule — every N days or a fixed weekday. As the due date approaches, the task fills up and shifts toward “needs attention.” Tap to mark it done and it resets.

How does sharing work?

Create a household and invite people with a code (House → Invite). Everyone in the household sees the same rooms and tasks update live.

How do I manage or restore my subscription?

Home Health Plus is billed through your Apple ID. Manage or cancel it in Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your device. To restore a previous purchase on a new device, open the upgrade screen in the app and tap Restore purchases.

How do I delete my account?

Open Profile → Delete account in the app. This permanently removes your account and personal data.

Reminders aren’t showing

Make sure notifications are allowed for Home Health in your device’s Settings, and that reminders are turned on in Profile.
