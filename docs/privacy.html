Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Home Health (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a household cleaning and upkeep tracker. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We keep it simple: we only collect what the app needs to work, and we never sell your data.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address to create and identify your account.
  • Profile. A display name and an avatar color you choose.
  • Household content. The households, rooms, cleaning tasks, schedules, and completion history you create, plus who belongs to a shared household.
  • Subscription status. Whether you have an active “Home Health Plus” subscription. Payments are processed by Apple; we never see your card details.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.

How we use it

  • To provide the core features: tracking tasks, computing your home‑health score and streaks, and syncing data across your devices.
  • To share tasks and progress with the members of a household you choose to join.
  • To unlock premium features for active subscribers.
  • To send optional local reminders when a task comes due (these are generated on your device).

How data is stored and shared

  • Storage. Your data is stored using Supabase (hosted database and authentication) and transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections.
  • Households. Content in a shared household is visible to the other members of that household. Don’t add people you don’t want to see your tasks.
  • Service providers. We rely on Apple and Google (sign‑in), Supabase (data storage), and RevenueCat (subscription management). They process data only to provide these services.
  • No selling, no ads. We do not sell your personal data and the app contains no third‑party advertising.

Your choices & data deletion

  • Delete your account. In the app, open Profile → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and personal data and cannot be undone.
  • You may also email us to request access to or deletion of your data.
  • You can disable reminders at any time in your device settings.

Children

Home Health is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
